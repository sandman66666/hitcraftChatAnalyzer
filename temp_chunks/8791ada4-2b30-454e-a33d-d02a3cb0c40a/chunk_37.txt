Here are a few steps you can take:

1. **Adjust the Levels**: Make sure the vocals are clear and sit well above the instrumental. You can do this by lowering the volume of the beat or increasing the vocal track.

2. **EQ the Vocals**: Use an equalizer to enhance the clarity of the vocals. Cutting some low frequencies and boosting the mid-high range can help the vocals stand out.

3. **Align the Flow**: If the flow doesn't match the beat, you might need to adjust the timing of the vocal track to better align with the instrumental.

4. **Compression**: Apply compression to the vocals to ensure they remain consistent in volume throughout the track.

5. **Reverb and Effects**: Add some reverb or delay to give the vocals a sense of space, but be careful not to overdo it.

If you need more detailed guidance on any of these steps, feel free to ask!"
