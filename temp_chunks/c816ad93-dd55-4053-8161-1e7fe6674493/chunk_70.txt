**7. Chorus:** Repeats, often with variations or added harmonies for intensity.
**8. Outro:** Concludes the song, sometimes reflecting the intro.

- **Chorus**: Catchy melody with the key message.

- **Chorus**: Reinforce the hook.
- **Bridge**: A reflective or contrasting moment.
- **Chorus**: Repeat with added layers (like harmonies).
