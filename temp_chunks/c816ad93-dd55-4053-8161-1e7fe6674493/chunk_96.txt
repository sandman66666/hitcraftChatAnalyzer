   - For slapback delay, go to **Effects > Delay and Echo > Delay**.

   - Select the vocal track, then go to **Effects > Filter and EQ > Parametric Equalizer**.

   - Go to **Effects > Amplitude and Compression > Dynamics Processing**.

6. **Volume Automation:**
   - Create a volume envelope by clicking on the track header, then select **Show Envelopes**.
   - Adjust the volume envelope to raise or lower the vocal level at specific points in the track as needed.

6. **Delete Keyframes:**
   - If you need to remove a keyframe, simply click on it to select it and hit the **Delete** key on your keyboard.
